The best meeting policy I've heard in a long time: Attendance is optional,
participation is mandatory.

In other words, show up and really show up, or don't show up and do something
better with your time. If you're coming, have your say, prepare, understand why
the meeting exists and the exit criteria. If you're not coming because you cant,
get someone to take notes or relay your input ahead of time. If you're not
coming because you have something better to do, great! Do the something better
